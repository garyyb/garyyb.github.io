Developer for UNSW Australia Robocup SPL Team

I am currently on the UNSW Australia Robocup team. Robocup Standard Platform League (SPL) is an international competition in the development of robots which are capable of autonomously playing soccer. UNSW fields a team consisted of undergraduate and postgraduate students.

I primarily work on the vision system. In 2016, I developed an automated colour classification system for the purpose of identifying green and white elements of the field. The system was written in C++. Development process included the use of libraries including OpenCV and Eigen.
